Rig : Berkut

Berkut boasts the biggest upper part in the world. Translated as ‘Golden Eagle’, Berkut is built to withstand subarctic conditions: including 60 foot waves, and temperatures of up to minus 44 degrees celsius. It is due to begin extracting oil in December 2014.
